CapSeg - Capture-based segmentation of copy number from cancer exomes.

CapSeg generates copy number information from cancer samples. It's goal is to produce allele specific copy information across the genome using matched tumor and normal samples, sequenced with whole-exome sequencing (WES). It is a produce of the Cancer Genome Analysis (CGA) group at the Broad Institute of Harvard and MIT.
